Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for passionate developers and has grown into one of the world’s leading technology conferences. Over the years, we've hosted thousands of attendees and speakers who shape the future of innovation.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to connect, inspire, and educate the global tech community. We aim to foster collaboration, showcase cutting-edge innovation, and empower individuals through knowledge-sharing and networking opportunities.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lin

Renowned AI researcher and author of "The Ethical Algorithm," Dr. Lin has contributed significantly to AI safety and machine learning ethics.

Marcus Reid

CEO of FutureWeb, Marcus is a pioneer in decentralized internet technologies and a frequent advocate for digital inclusion and access.

Sophia Nakamura

Leading robotics engineer and inventor of modular robotics systems used in space exploration. Sophia has been a keynote speaker since 2019.
